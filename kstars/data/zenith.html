The Zenith

The Zenith is the point in the sky where you are looking when you look "straight up". More precisely, it is the point on the sky with an Altitude of 90 degrees, so it is the pole of the Horizontal coordinate system. Geometrically, it is the point on the celestial sphere intersected by a line drawn from the center of the Earth through your location on its surface.

The Zenith is, by definition, a point along the Local Meridian.

Exercise: You can find the Zenith point by selecting Zenith from the Location Menu.
